Crafts at Stellmar Farm



Stellmar Farm carries many hand-crafted items made right on the farm. Our gourd art is a long process because the gourds require at least one full year to dry to a point where they can be used. These everlasting pieces of art will keep you company for many years to come.


The pine cone wreaths are a family tradition. The unique process developed by Joseph’s dad ensures that the wreaths are well made and durable. We have a wreath that is over 30 years old and it still looks great!
