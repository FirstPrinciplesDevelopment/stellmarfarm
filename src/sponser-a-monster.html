Sponser A Monster 2022



*UPDATED* Congratulations to Pie Eyed Monk for winning the top spot in the Sponsor a Monster vote for the second year in a row. Their Giant Fright Man was a big hit. Second place went to The Port Perry Animal Hospital with their Vet Problem. Third spot went to Burn's Bulk Food with their spider arch. Thank you to all of the companies and families that placed monsters in the maze and thanks also to everyone who voted. We will be notifying winners of the prizes very soon.

Stellmar Farm invites any local businesses to participate in our Sponsor A Monster event. There is no cost other than cost of materials. If you are interested, send a message to info@stellmarfarm.com stating your intention to participate. We will respond with the official acceptance.

Details:

  • Come up with an idea for a monster that will be installed in our corn maze from September 17th until Halloween.
  • Create the monster with dimensions: 6-7 feet tall and maximum 3 feet wide. Keep thickness (or depth) to about 1 foot if possible.
  • Complete the monster and delivery it to Stellmar Farm by September 10th.
  • You can include your business information in the display.
